Bread

Description

This is a recipe for homemade bread.

This recipe yields a soft and fluffy loaf.

    Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Warm Water
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Butter

    Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and melted butter to the yeast mixture and knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Shape the dough into a loaf and place it in a greased bread pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped.
  7. Let the bread cool before slicing.
