Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for the World's Best Lasagna.

This recipe will have your familiy and friends coming back for more.

    Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Egg

    Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
