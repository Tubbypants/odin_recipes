Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for homemade pizza.

This recipe is easy to make and delicious.

    Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Warm Water
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

    Steps

  1. Make the dough by combining yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it sit until frothy.
  2. Add flour and salt to the yeast mixture and knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough and place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  6. Spread tomato sauce over the dough and add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
